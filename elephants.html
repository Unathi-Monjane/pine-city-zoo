Elephants

Gentle giants of the jungle.

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, complex social structures, and strong family bonds. At Pine City Zoo, our elephants enjoy spacious habitats that simulate their natural environment, where visitors can observe their fascinating behaviors.
